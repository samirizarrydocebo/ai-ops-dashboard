Industry formulas for signal triangulation
1
The 3-source rule

A buying signal is only actionable when confirmed by 3+ independent data sources. Single-source signals lead to false positives ~60% of the time.

SourcesConfidenceAction
1 sourceLow (30-40%)Monitor only
2 sourcesMedium (50-70%)Qualify further
3+ sourcesHigh (80-95%)Act immediately

Each tool provides a unique signal type. Combining them creates a full picture of account intent and readiness.

Triangulation formula applied

Confidence % = (number of confirming sources / 5) x base_weight + recency_bonus + stakeholder_seniority_bonus. Base weight = 40 for 1 source, 60 for 2, 80 for 3, 90 for 4, 95 for 5. Recency bonus = +5 if signal within 14 days. Stakeholder bonus = +5 if VP+ mentioned the signal. This approach follows Forrester's B2B signal triangulation methodology and aligns with Docebo's MEDDPICC qualification framework.
