What is a "skill"?

  • Reusable AI capabilities (prompt + logic packages) that agents can call
  • Users invoke powerful workflows via simple commands
  • No need to be expert prompt engineers
  • Skills chain multiple steps (e.g., "run health report", "summarize risk drivers", "draft outreach")

Planned core skills

  • Enterprise Search: Tuned to Docebo's stack and values
  • Brand Guidelines: Marketing-approved messaging and tone
  • Health Report: Customer health scoring and narratives
  • Risk Summary: Account risk identification and drivers
  • Outreach Drafts: Personalized customer communications

Domain hand-off model

  • Phase 1: AI Ops builds the first 4-10 generic skills
  • Phase 2: Ownership transitions to domain experts (Brand, CS, Product)
  • Ongoing: Domain teams obsess over content and accuracy
  • AI Ops role: Maintains underlying infrastructure and patterns
